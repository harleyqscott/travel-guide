Relaxing Holiday Destination 1

Budapest ~ Hungary

Best time to visit: September ~ Ocober and March ~ May

Best For... Budapest is famous for its elegant, unique architecture, the local castle and its funicular, and legendary Roman-style hot baths. It is also known for its underground cave network, colorful nightlife, and rich, multinational history. Other reasons to visit Budapest include its vibrant food scene, fairytale-like appearance, and greenery.

Must See... Soak at one of the city's incredible thermal spas, Explore the Castle District, Stroll along Andrássy Avenue and in City Park, Have a coffee at a historical coffeehouse, See the city from a viewpoint or rooftop bar, Spend a day on Margaret Island, See the distinctive architecture of the Great Synagogue, Experience the best nightlife at ruin pubs and garden clubs, Explore history in Memento Park and Ride the rails in the Buda Hills.

Top Restaurant... Leo Rooftop Budapest-Bar & International, Retek Bisztro-Hungarian & Vegetarian friendly, Beerstro 14-Steakhouse & Hungarian, Mazel Tov-Mediterranean & European, La Pampa Steakhouse-Steakhouse & Gluten free options and Twentysix-Mediterranean & European.

Relaxing Holiday Destination 3

Montego Bay ~ Jamaica

Best time to visit: December - April

Best For... Vibrant nightlife, Golden sand beaches and calm waters, Excellent duty-free shopping, A cruise line terminal, Watch planes fly from the sand, Hang out at Dump Up Beach, Enjoy water sports like parasailing, jet-skiing, and boat rides, Get up close and personal with marine life, Swim with horses, Go on safari and Play golf.

Must See... Take the cure at Doctor's Cave Beach, Jimmy Buffett's Margaritaville(water park, restaurant, and nightclub all in one), Ras Natango Gallery and Garden, Sam Sharpe Square, Rocklands Bird Sanctuary, YS Falls, Mystic Mountain, Mayfield Falls and Croydon Plantation.

Top Restaurant... The Houseboat Grill-Caribbean & Seafood, Scotchies-Caribbean & Barbecue, Seagrape Terrace-Caribbean & Jamaican, Pier - 1 On The Waterfront-Caribbean & Bar, The Pelican Grill-Caribbean & Jamaican and Jimmy Buffett's Margaritaville-American & Caribbean.
