Travel Guide

Wanting a Winter Holiday? Then this is the page for you!

Winter Holiday Destination 1

Grindelwald ~ Switzerland

Best time to visit: June - October

Best For... Skiing, Snowbaording, Mountain biking, Hiking and Sledging.

Must See... First Cliff Walk, Eiger Trail, Jungfraujoch Mountain, Bachalpsee, Pfingstegg Valley, Gletscherschlucht Ravine and Grindelwald Museum.

Top Restaurant... Barrys Restaurant, Bar & Lounge-Swiss & bar, Restaurant Kreuz & Post-Swiss, Restaurant Belvedere-Swiss & International, Avacado Bar-Bar & Pub, Restaurant Pinte-Swiss & Central European, Ristorante Pizzeria Da Salvi-Italian & Pizza.

Winter Holiday Destination 2

Innsbruck ~ Austria

Best time to visit: April - October

Best For... Skiing and resorts, Historic landmarks from the Habsburg Empire including baroque-style Hofburg Palace, Gothic Hofkirche Church, Ambras Castle and the Golden Roof.

Must See... Cross the Innbrucke, Climb the Town Tower (Stadtturm), See the Golden Roof (Goldenes Dachl) and Goldenes Dachl Museum, Visit the Court Church (Hofkirche), Take the Funicular to Hungerburg, Seegrube and the 'Top of Innsbruck', Visit the Alpine Zoo (Alpenzoo Innsbruck), Wander Old Town Innsbruck and Experience the 'Top of Innsbruck' from the Nordkette.

Top Restaurant... Le Fief Restaurant-Italian & Mediterranean, Mr. Pickwick Pub Luzern-Bar & European, Stiftskeller-Austrian classics, Gasthaus Anich-rustic Tyrolean food Ottoburg-Austrian gourmet food Himal-Nepalese cuisine.

Winter Holiday Destination 3

Amsterdam ~ Netherlands

Best time to visit: October - February

Best For... Canal belt, flower market, canal houses, coffee shops, Red Light District, LGBTI scene, and party scene.

Must See... Amsterdam: AMAZE Immersive Audiovisual Experience, Icebar, Traditional Dutch Syrup Waffle Making Workshop, Heineken Experience, Anne Frank House, Van Gogh Museum, Amsterdam Dungeon and the A'DAM Lookout.

Top Restaurant... Fulu Mandarijn Amsterdam-Chinese & Asian, The Pantry-Dutch & International, Midtown Grill-Steakhouse, MAX Amsterdam-Contemporary & Indonesian, Day's StoneGrill 1870, -Steakhouse & International, De Belhamel-French & Dutch.

Winter Holiday Destination 4

Lapland ~ Finland

Best time to visit: November - March

Best For... Northern Lights,The Santa Claus Village, skiing, Sami culture in Inari, Husky or reindeer sledding, Icehotel, Snowshoeing or snowmobiling and Norwegian Lapland.

Must See... Northan Lights, Visiting the Santa Claus Village, Husky sledding, Hiking through the Korouoma canyon, Snowshoeing, Camping in Ruska, Experiencing a reindeer safari, Visiting an ice hotel and Lappish cuisine

Top Restaurant... Restaurant Aanaar-European & Scandinavian, Santa's Salmon Place-Barbecue & European, Aurora Estate Restaurant-Scandinavian & Vegetarian friendly Yllashumina, Humina Restaurant-European & Scandinavian, Laanilan kievari-European & Scandinavian, Lapland Restaurant Kotahovi-European & Scandinavian.

Winter Holiday Destination 5

Riga ~ Latvia

Best time to visit: October, February and April

Best For... Impressive architecture, Fantastic nightlife, Riga has a picture-perfect Old Town, Fewer tourists, World-class food scene, Quirky Soviet Heritage and Flourishing art scene

Must See... Riga Cathedral, The Beauty of the House of the Black Heads, the viewpoint at the top of St. Peter's Church, Walk through The Swedish Gate, Museums in Riga, The Freedom Monument and The Cathedral of the Nativity of Christ.

Top Restaurant... Milda-European & Balti, Two More Beers-Bar & European Gastro Pub Riga Duvel's-Belgian & Pub, LIDO Atputas Centrs-European & Latvian, Belgian Beer Cafe Bon Vivant-Belgian & Pub, Bella Napoli-Italian & Pizza.

Winter Holiday Destination 6

Morzine ~ French Alps

Best time to visit: December - January

Best For... Vast ski terrain, Historic charm, vibrant apres-ski culture and ski resorts.

Must See... Avoriaz(Ski & Snowboard Areas), Le Lac de Montriond(Bodies of Water), Les Lindarets Village des Chevres(Neighbourhoods), ESF Avoriaz 1800(Ski & Snowboard Areas), Ski With Ease(Sports Camps & Clinics - Ski & Snowboard Areas) and La Ferme de Seraussaix(Farms).

Top Restaurant... Le Clin D'Oeil-French & European, La Grange-French & European, Bec Jaune Brewery-Brew Pub & Bar, L'Etale-Italian & French, La Chaudanne-French & European.
